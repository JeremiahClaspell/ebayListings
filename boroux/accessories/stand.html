Stainless Steel Stand ‚Äî FAQs

Questions about fit, installation, care and compatibility of the stainless steel stand accessory.

What does the stand do?
The stainless steel stand elevates your BOROUX system providing extra cup clearance, better ergonomics for pouring, and improved stability for the lower chamber.
What materials is the stand made of?
Constructed from 304-grade stainless steel with brushed finish. No plastic feet or brackets touch the main body‚Äîjust stainless and high-density rubber pads for floor protection.
Which BOROUX systems is it compatible with?
Compatible with BOROUX Legacy and Compact systems. Also fits most other stainless gravity water systems with a base diameter up to ~11¬Ω‚Ä≥ (29 cm).
How difficult is the assembly?
Assembly takes under 5 minutes using the included hex-key and hardware. The stand ships flat-packed; just unfold legs, attach mounting bolts, and level with pads.
What is the height of the stand?
The stand raises the system by approximately 5.5 inches (14 cm), giving more clearance for pitchers, jugs, and tall cups underneath the spigot.
Does the stand affect flow or performance?
No. The stand only elevates the system ‚Äì it neither restricts flow nor alters filtration performance of your gravity system.
How do I clean and maintain the stand?
Wipe with a damp cloth and mild dish soap if needed. Avoid harsh abrasive cleaners or steel wool which may scratch the finish. Rubber pads can be removed for deeper cleaning.
Will the stand corrode or rust?
The 304-grade stainless steel is highly resistant to rust and corrosion in normal home conditions. If used outdoors or in humid environments, rinse periodically and dry to maintain the finish.
Does it come with leveling feet?
Yes. The stand includes four adjustable rubber-damped feet so you can stabilize it on uneven surfaces and protect your countertop.

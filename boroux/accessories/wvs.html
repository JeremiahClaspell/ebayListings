No. The spigot uses stainless steel and ceramic for taste-neutral, long-life performance.
How do I stop a slow drip at the spout?
Rotate the handle to fully closed. If dripping persists, back off, then re-close to re-seat the ceramic discs. Check that the inner nut is snug and gaskets are flat.

Disassemble, rinse parts, and wash with mild soap. Avoid abrasives on stainless. Rinse the tube with warm water; let air-dry before reassembly.
Can I run hot water through it?

Yes‚Äîspigot assembly, inner/outer gaskets, washer(s), nut, and level-tube hardware are included.

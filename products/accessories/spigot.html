Specs & Fit

  • Materials: 304 stainless steel body & nut; ceramic valve core; stainless trim/washer stack.
  • Plastic Content: none (0%).
  • Hole Size: fits standard gravity outlet (≈ 1/2″ / 12–13 mm).
  • Finish: brushed stainless.
  • Includes: spigot assembly, inner/outer gaskets, stainless nut.
  • Care: hand-rinse; mild soap as needed; avoid abrasives.

2-Minute Install

  1. Prep: Empty lower chamber. Remove old spigot and clean the outlet hole.
  2. Mount: Insert stainless spigot from outside → add gasket/washer inside → hand-tighten nut, then quarter-turn to seal. Fill and leak-check.
